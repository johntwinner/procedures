Expectation and Procedures:

  • Be prepared! Bring pencil, paper, charged laptop, etc.
  • Stow cell phones and other personal electronics - they will be confiscated otherwise!
  1. Turn in assignments at the door
  2. If you did not complete your assignment, fill out an assignment responsibility form
  3. Retrieve your engineering binder
  4. Store your backpack under the table
  5. Review agenda in Echo
  6. Do the warm-up activity
  • Verify the area is clean and all materials replaced
  • The instructor dismisses you, not the clock!
  • Stay seated and quiet until the instructor releases you
  • When released, push chairs in and exit in a controlled manner
  • When the instructor calls the class to attention, stop what you are doing and give him your respect and attention
  • Do not remove anything from the instructor's desk area
  • Wait to be called before responding
  • When help is needed, ask your peers first
  • Refer to Polizzi's Principles for additional information
  • Assemble teams promptly when instructed to do so
  • Participate in the assigned effort without being disruptive
  • Keep discussions on-topic. Less chat, more churn!
  • Water is only allowed in the classroom with a lid on
  • No food/drink in the shop area whatsoever
  • Place trash in trash cans; recycle if possible
  • Notify instructor of any breakage, non-functioning equipment, spills, or injuries
  • Plan to use the bathroom outside of class time
  • Get instructor permission before going to the bathroom
  • Only one person will be allowed in the bathroom at a time
  • Sign out and sign in when going to the bathroom
  • Provide the instructor with a tardy slip without interrupting the class
  • Review Echo for assignments. Check data box for handouts.
